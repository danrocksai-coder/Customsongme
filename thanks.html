Because each song is custom-made, real time is spent making sure it reflects your story rather than a generic template. That is what makes it special.

If you included strong details like names, memories, inside jokes, tone, or specific moments, your final song will feel more personal and accurate to what you had in mind.

Payment for Personal Songs

Your custom personal song is created under a pay what you feel model. There is no upfront requirement for personal song requests.

If you enjoy the final result and feel it was worth something to you, you can support through:

  • PayPal
  • Cash App

Payment is never required for personal songs, but it directly supports the ability to keep creating for others.
