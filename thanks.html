What Happens Next

Your song will be carefully written and produced based on the details you submitted. Every song is made to feel personal, meaningful, and unique to you.

Delivery is within 7 days. In many cases, it may arrive sooner depending on current request volume.

Important Notes

Because each song is custom-made, time is spent making sure it actually reflects your story, not just a generic template. That is what makes it special.

If you included strong details like names, memories, inside jokes, tone, or specific moments, your final song will feel more personal and accurate.

Payment & Support

Your custom personal song is created under a pay-what-you-feel model. There is no upfront requirement for personal song requests.

If you enjoy the final result and feel it was worth something to you, you will have the option to support through:

  • PayPal
  • Cash App
  • Venmo

Tips are never required, but they directly support the ability to keep creating songs for others and help keep this offer going.

Limited Daily Capacity: Each custom song takes real time to write and produce, so only a limited number of requests can be completed each day. If your song is for a birthday, event, surprise, or deadline, tipping helps move your request higher in the queue when volume is high.

Business Jingles

If your request is for a business, brand, promotion, or commercial use, those projects start at a $50 minimum because they carry added business value.

Need to Add or Fix Something?

If you forgot something important or want to update your request, reply to your confirmation email as soon as possible before your song is finalized.

Final Note

This is not generic output. It is built from your input. The more real your story is, the better your song will be.
