Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I tip for personal songs?

It’s up to you! We use a Pay What You Feel It’s Worth model for personal songs (birthdays, love songs, pets, anniversaries, etc.). Most people give $20–$30, but any amount helps. $5+ tips get priority delivery.

Do business jingles cost the same as personal songs?

No — business jingles are separate because they require more work and deliver real value for your brand.

  • Minimum price: $50 (paid upfront).
  • Delivery: typically within 12–24 hours after payment + order ID confirmation.
  • Rights: You’ll receive a professional MP3 you can use for ads, social media, or branding.

If you have questions about jingles, email me directly at danrocksai@gmail.com.

How fast will I get my personal song?

Most songs are delivered within 24–48 hours. Tippers ($5+) usually get theirs faster. If the queue is busy, non-tippers may wait longer.

Can I pick the genre?

Yes — you can choose from hip-hop, pop, R&B, country, acoustic, or “surprise me.”

➡️ And if you don’t see the genre you want, select Other and write it in.

What do I receive?

  • MP3 delivered by email (WAV available on request).
  • Personalized lyrics and vocals.
  • Up to 3 free revisions.
  • For jingles: professional-grade output optimized for your business.

What if I’m not satisfied?

You get up to 3 free revisions. If it’s not right, I’ll make changes until it feels like your story — or your brand — is captured in the music. For business jingles only: after the 3 revisions, if you’re still not completely satisfied, there’s a 100% money-back guarantee.
